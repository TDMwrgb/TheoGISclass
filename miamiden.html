Population Density of Northern Miami

Exploring urban patterns through GIS

Posted by Theo McGreevey on June 25, 2025

In this post, I explore the population density of Northern Miami using recent census and GIS data. Areas closer to downtown show significantly higher density compared to outlying neighborhoods.

The map highlights how density varies across neighborhoods and helps us understand urban development and zoning implications. This analysis is part of my GIS final lab work.
