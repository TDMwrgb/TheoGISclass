Chicago Homicides

Map and Analysis of Police Districts

This map shows the distribution of homicides across police districts in Chicago. The Chicago city government was worried that perceptions of crime were driving away tourism. I needed to make 2 choropleths of Chicago to help visualize where homicide cases are concentrated and where Airbnb’s are concentrated in the city. For the assignment I also needed to make a table of Airbnb prices by police district. The Airbnb and homicide layers contained hundreds of individual points, while there were only 23 police districts, so I used ArcGis's Summary tool to group the Airbnb’s and homicides by district so that that I could then calculate their concentrations by district. I also needed to get data from one table to another to do my calculations, so I used the join tool. I made this map during mt summer online GIS class with professor Bradley Gardener.
